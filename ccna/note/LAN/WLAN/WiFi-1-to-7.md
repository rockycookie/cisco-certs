# WiFi versions

- 802.11b (WiFi 1)
    - 3 non-overlapping channels
    - 2.4 GHz
    - max 11 Mbps
- 802.11a (WiFi 2)
    - 23 channels
    - 5 GHz
    - max 54 Mbps
- 802.11ac (WiFi 5)
    - 23+ channels
    - 5 GHz
    - max 6.9 Gbps
- 802.11ax (WiFi 6)
    - 23+ channels
    - both 2.4 and 5 GHz
    - max 9.6 Gbps
- 802.11be (WiFi 7)
    - 23+ channels even more
    - 2.4, 5 or 6 GHz
    - max 23 Gbps
